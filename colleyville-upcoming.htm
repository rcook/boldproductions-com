.Logistics:    
 

.
Colleyville, Texas, is located due west of DFW Airport. Its major roads are Hwy. 121 and Hwy. 26. Access them from Hwy. 114 at the airport's north exit.

Colleyville Center is located at 5301 Riverwalk Dr., one long block east of Hwy. 26, also known as Colleyville Rd. The Center is at the dead end of Church St., at a point about mid-way between the major roads of Glade and Hall-Johnson.
