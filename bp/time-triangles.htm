Time triangles

     

Left: New students spend more time than they expect learning the technology, adjusting to a learner-centered environment, and finally absorbing course content. The have little time for community-building.

Right: Veteran students need little time to adapt to course technology and teaching methods. They spend the bulk of their time in course content and community-building.
