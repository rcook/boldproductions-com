  Other Courses
    FS 5533 Parenting Education is one of eleven courses that comprise the Online Master's in Family Studies. The course is also open to other majors.

Graduate course offerings of the Department of Family Sciences include:

100% online courses (with optional on-campus seminars 1 time per semester)
partially online courses (meet on campus 2 - 7 times per semester)
on-campus weekly classes (meet 14 - 15 times per semester)
on-campus weekend classes (meet 4 - 5 times per semester)
