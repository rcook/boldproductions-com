Creating An Assessment Culture at TWU
.
Goals for Learning Outcomes Assessment:
Do it because it’s best for our students and not because the CB or SACS says we must.
Agree as a faculty on learning and skills standards for all our graduates.

Construct and maintain information systems that can

track changes over time.
tell us if changes make a difference.
 communicate clearly about student performance at all levels.
Use assessment results to allocate resources.
